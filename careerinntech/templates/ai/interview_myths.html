❌ Myths & Mistakes

Avoid Common Interview Traps

Many candidates lose points due to avoidable misconceptions. Fix these before your next interview.

Mindset Clarity Concise Honest

Myth: Perfect answers only

Interviewers value thinking, clarity, and honesty over a perfect script.

Myth: You must know everything

Admit gaps and explain how you’d find the answer.

Mistake: Talking too long

Keep responses focused. Use structure to avoid rambling.

Mistake: Ignoring fundamentals

Strong basics beat memorized solutions. Revisit core topics.

Quick Fixes

Use checkpoints

Summarize after each answer: “So the key point is…”

It keeps the interviewer aligned with your logic.

Ask clarifying questions

It’s better to confirm than to guess.

Clarify scope, constraints, and success criteria.

Close with impact

Finish with outcomes, numbers, or what you learned.

Use results to be memorable.

Avoid These Habits

Overusing buzzwords

Replace with clear examples and outcomes.

Blaming teams

Show ownership and what you learned.

Skipping basics

Review fundamentals weekly to stay sharp.
