Understand the Interview Landscape

Every stage has its own expectations. Learn what each round tests so you can prepare with focus.

HR Technical System Design Managerial Case Study

HR / Behavioral

Checks your attitude, culture fit, and motivation. Expect stories about leadership, conflict, and growth.

  • Tell me about yourself
  • Why this company?
  • Strengths & weaknesses

Technical (Core + DSA)

Tests fundamentals, algorithms, and problem-solving approach. Explain your thinking clearly.

  • Data structures & algorithms
  • Core CS concepts
  • Time/space trade-offs

System Design

Assesses scalability, architecture, and decision-making. Think end-to-end.

  • Requirements ‚Üí architecture
  • Trade-offs and bottlenecks
  • APIs, data, and scale

Managerial / Leadership

Focuses on collaboration, stakeholder handling, and ownership of outcomes.

  • Decision-making examples
  • Handling pressure
  • Team communication

What to Do Next

Map your target role

Shortlist companies and role level to estimate which rounds you'll likely face.

Build a checklist of round types for each company.

Prepare in sequence

Start with HR, then technical, then design. It builds confidence fast.

Use 1-2 weeks per category for focused practice.

Practice out loud

Speaking clearly matters as much as correct answers.

Record 3 answers and refine them daily.
