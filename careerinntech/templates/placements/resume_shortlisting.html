Resume & Shortlisting

Craft a resume that clears ATS and recruiter filters.

Resume shortlisting is where most candidates are filtered out. A concise, impact‑driven resume with the right keywords drastically improves your chances.

Resume Structure

  • One-page impact layout
  • Keyword alignment
  • Relevant achievements
  • Consistent formatting
  • Clear section hierarchy

Projects Section

  • Problem → Solution → Result
  • Metrics & outcomes
  • Tech stack clarity
  • Link to demos or GitHub
  • Use action verbs

Shortlisting Tips

  • Tailor to job role
  • Highlight internships
  • Proofread & format
  • Avoid irrelevant skills
  • Show leadership/impact

ATS Checklist

  • Use standard section titles
  • Simple fonts, no tables
  • Save as PDF

Common Mistakes

  • Too much fluff
  • Unverified claims
  • Inconsistent dates

Profile Summary

  • 2–3 lines max
  • Role + key strengths
  • Career objective clarity
