Computer Science (CS)

Computer Science focuses on computation, software systems, algorithms, and the theoretical foundations of modern technology. It is one of the most in-demand and versatile engineering branches.

What You Will Study

  • Programming Languages (C, C++, Java, Python)
  • Data Structures and Algorithms
  • Operating Systems
  • Database Management Systems
  • Computer Networks
  • Software Engineering

Skills You Will Build

  • Strong problem-solving and logical thinking
  • Software development and debugging
  • System design and optimization
  • Understanding large-scale applications

Future Scope

  • High demand in product-based and service companies
  • Excellent opportunities in startups and MNCs
  • Strong base for AI, ML, Data Science, and Cloud
  • Opportunities for higher studies and research

Career Opportunities

  • Software Engineer / Developer
  • Backend / Full Stack Engineer
  • Data Scientist
  • AI / ML Engineer
  • Systems Engineer
  • Research Engineer
