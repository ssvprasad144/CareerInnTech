Electrical Engineering (EE)

Electrical Engineering deals with the study and application of electricity, power systems, electrical machines, and control systems. It is a core engineering branch essential for energy, infrastructure, and industrial development.

What You Will Study

  • Electrical Circuits & Networks
  • Electrical Machines
  • Power Generation & Transmission
  • Power Electronics
  • Control Systems
  • Renewable Energy Systems

Skills You Will Build

  • Power system analysis
  • Design and testing of electrical machines
  • Working with industrial electrical equipment
  • Understanding control and automation systems

Future Scope

  • Strong demand in power and energy sectors
  • Key role in renewable energy and smart grids
  • Opportunities in government and PSU jobs
  • Foundation for higher studies and research

Career Opportunities

  • Electrical Engineer
  • Power Systems Engineer
  • Control & Automation Engineer
  • Energy Analyst
  • Maintenance & Plant Engineer
